Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need roofing repair or a full roof replacement?

The decision between repair and replacement depends on several factors including the age of your roof, extent of damage, and overall condition. Generally, if your roof is under 15 years old with localized damage, repair is cost-effective. However, if your roof is older, has widespread damage, multiple leaks, or significant structural issues, replacement may be the better long-term investment. Our team provides honest assessments and will always recommend the most economical solution for your situation.

How quickly can you respond to emergency roof repairs?

We understand that roofing emergencies require immediate attention. Our emergency roof repair team is available to respond quickly, often within hours of your call. We prioritize storm damage, active leaks, and situations that threaten your property's interior. While we provide temporary solutions to stop further damage immediately, we'll also schedule permanent repairs as soon as weather and materials permit.

What types of roofing materials do you work with?

We work with all major roofing materials suitable for South Florida's climate, including asphalt shingles, clay and concrete tiles, metal roofing, flat roof systems (TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen), and more. Each material has unique benefits in terms of durability, energy efficiency, cost, and appearance. During your consultation, we'll explain the pros and cons of each option and help you select the best roofing material for your property, budget, and aesthetic preferences.

How long does a typical roof repair or replacement take?

Timeline varies based on project scope and complexity. Most roof repairs can be completed in one day, while minor repairs may take just a few hours. A complete roof replacement for an average-sized residential property typically takes 2-5 days, depending on roof size, complexity, material choice, and weather conditions. Commercial projects may take longer. We provide detailed timelines during your estimate and keep you informed throughout the project.

Do you offer warranties on your roofing work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. All our roofing installations come with manufacturer warranties on materials (typically 20-50 years depending on the product) plus our own workmanship warranty covering installation quality. Specific warranty terms vary by project type and materials used. We'll clearly explain all warranty coverage before beginning your project, giving you peace of mind and long-term protection for your investment.
